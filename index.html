About Where’s Spot

Where’s Spot is a website that helps man’s best friend find new friends; we help dogs connect to each other. With our state of the art locator a dog can make new friends without the awkward interaction of their owners.

When dog owners are trying to schedule play date for their dogs, it can get difficult to approach other dog owner. Where’s Spot, helps skip this process. This website allows you to message different dog owners and get in contact with them before meeting in person; we act as an ice breaker.

Activities are always fun when they are done with friends. Where’s Spot can help a dog connect to new friends while at the park or when shopping. Making new friends means more dog parties, Bring out the dog biscuits!!!!!
